Knowledge



  • Salicylic acid is a common skincare ingredient known for its acne-fighting and exfoliating properties. It penetrates deep into pores, unclogs them, and dissolves excess oil, making it effective for treating acne, blackheads, and whiteheads. Salicylic acid also regulates oil production and has anti-inflammatory properties. While it's widely available in over-the-counter products, it's essential to start with lower concentrations to avoid irritation and sun sensitivity.

  • Hylamide is a skincare brand offering advanced formulations targeting various skin concerns. Developed by DECIEM, it features innovative ingredients and technologies for improved skin health and appearance.
